Motivation

(1) Develop a design generation engine for PowerPoint and Microsoft Designer. (2) Generate high-quality design data for Responsible VL models to understand business content such as posters, flyers, and other materials. (3) DALLE-3 is weak at generating high-quality graphic designs.



Key Challenges

DALL-E 3 frequently struggles with generating accurate graphic designs due to its limited ability to grasp true intentions and manage fine-grained controls, such as counting-aware or spatial-relationship aware prompts and accurately render visual text.
